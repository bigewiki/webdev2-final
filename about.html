About

Meet our family

Hello! Thank you for visiting the site. The picture here has our two dogs, Jazzy and Penny. They're both mutts, we can only speculate to their breeds, not that it matters much. They’re both quirky and sweet in their own regard. Jasmine is the one with the brindle coat, the older of the two at only three years old. She loves to bark at shadows and go on w-a-l-k’s (you can’t say the word out loud or she’ll get very excited). Penny, the other with the black coat, is the puppy at only 5 months old. She’s a bundle of boundless energy who loves to run, jump, dig, and relieve anything stuffed of its inner fluff. The dog bed pictured has since met its brutal end in a scene of fluffy carnage.

When it comes to dog training, I’m no professional. I’ve made mistakes and have got a lot of work to do. When I originally chose this topic for my website Penny was a brand new addition to our household. I was watching training videos frequently and doing my best to stay consistent. It’s a challenge a lot of the time but I expect the work and effort to pay off in the long run with a well mannered and behaved dog.

This website will act as a conduit for everything I’ve learned over the past few months in my endeavors with the basics puppy training. Putting all this together helps solidify the information and brings me back to the basics, which still need some work. Hopefully this helps you too.

* I’m not an expert, any advice or suggestions I give should be reviewed objectively and validated through your own research. I have the best intentions but I claim no responsibility for any harm or damage to anyone or anything through following what I’ve written on this site.
