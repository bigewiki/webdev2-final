New Puppies

The thought of adding a new furry addition to the family can be both exciting and daunting. We're often unprepared as new pet owners, even if we've done it. Start here if you've gotten a new puppy or plan on getting one.

Training

Training your puppy is important, not only to have a well-behaved dog, but for their own safety and everyone’s sanity. There’s a handful of basics every dog should know and all new puppies owners should take the time to work on some fundamentals with their family member.

About

Learn about me and my family! I'm not a professional trainer but wanted to pass on what I've learned during my struggle to train our new puppy. It hasn’t been easy and we’ve had to adapt to our newest family member with boundless energy.
